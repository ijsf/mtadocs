A [ban](/docs/ban.md "wikilink") is a pointer that represents a banned player arbitrarily. The following functions allow interaction with a [ban](/ban.md "wikilink"):

-   [addBan](/docs/addBan.md "wikilink")
-   [banPlayer](/docs/banPlayer.md "wikilink")
-   [getBans](/docs/getBans.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanAdmin](/docs/getBanAdmin.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanIP](/docs/getBanIP.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanReason](/docs/getBanReason.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanSerial](/docs/getBanSerial.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanTime](/docs/getBanTime.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanUsername](/docs/getBanUsername.md "wikilink")
-   [removeBan](/docs/removeBan.md "wikilink")
