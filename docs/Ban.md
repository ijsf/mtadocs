A [ban](/ban.md "wikilink") is a pointer that represents a banned player arbitrarily. The following functions allow interaction with a [ban](/ban.md "wikilink"):

-   [addBan](/addBan.md "wikilink")
-   [banPlayer](/banPlayer.md "wikilink")
-   [getBans](/getBans.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanAdmin](/getBanAdmin.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanIP](/getBanIP.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanReason](/getBanReason.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanSerial](/getBanSerial.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanTime](/getBanTime.md "wikilink")
-   [getBanUsername](/getBanUsername.md "wikilink")
-   [removeBan](/removeBan.md "wikilink")
